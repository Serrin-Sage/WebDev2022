The Legend of Zelda
The Legend of Zelda is my all time favorite video game franchise. I grew up playing the N64 games and ever since I've played each new iteration. The first zelda game was released in 1986 and it continues to grow today. There is a new new game currently in the works and may be released sometime this year or next. The main character you play as is Link, an unexpected hero of the story he's involved in. Each game you play as Link but each Link is not the same, they are spirtual decendants of "The Hero" reincarnated into the Link you would be playing as.
